We Are Developing a Safer, Inclusive Community for Our Elderly

Muzita Legacy is a Private Voluntary Organisation supporting Zimbabwe's elderly with care and community. We provide home visits, health support, and social connections for those needing dignity and companionship.

Contact Us Learn More

What We Do

Comprehensive support services to empower our elderly community

Home Visits & Companionship

Regular home visits providing companionship, emotional support, and social connection for elderly community members.

Health Checks & Medication Support

Health monitoring, medication reminders, and coordination with healthcare providers to maintain wellness.

Pension Advocacy & Food Aid

Assistance with pension access and provision of food aid to ensure nutritional security for vulnerable seniors.

Caregiver Training

Capacity building programs for family caregivers and community leaders to improve elderly care skills.

Support Groups

Establishing community support groups for caregivers and elderly individuals to share experiences and support.

Community Outreach

Engaging communities in elderly care through awareness programs and volunteer opportunities.
