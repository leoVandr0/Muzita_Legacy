About Muzita Legacy

MUZITA is a Private Voluntary Organisation supporting Zimbabwe's elderly with care & community. We provide home visits, health support & social connections for those needing dignity & companionship.

Our Vision

Empowering a compassionate community that values and supports the elderly, promoting dignity, respect and inclusivity for all.

Our Mission

To provide holistic care and support to the elderly in our community, empowering caregivers and fostering a sense of social responsibility while ensuring long sustainability and impact.

Improve Quality of Life

Enhance physical, emotional and spiritual wellbeing of elderly community members through comprehensive support services.

Empower Caregivers

Equip caregivers with skills, knowledge and confidence to provide better care for elderly family members and community members.

Promote Community Engagement

Foster sense of community and social responsibility through outreach programs and volunteer opportunities.

Ensure Sustainability

Ensure long term sustainability through resource mobilization and partnership development with stakeholders and donors.

Community Support

Build strong support networks that connect elderly individuals with community resources and social connections.

Advocacy & Rights

Champion the rights and interests of elderly individuals, ensuring they receive the care, respect, and services they deserve.

Our Activities

Muzita Legacy implements a range of activities designed to support and empower elderly community members and their caregivers. Our programs are built on the principle that elderly care is a shared community responsibility.

Through our established support groups, home-based care services, and capacity building programs, we create sustainable solutions that address the unique challenges faced by Zimbabwe's elderly population. We believe in fostering independence while providing the necessary support for dignity and quality of life.

Our community outreach and engagement activities help build awareness about elderly care issues and mobilize community resources to create a more inclusive and supportive environment for our senior citizens.
