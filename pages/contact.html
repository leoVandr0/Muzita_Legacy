Frequently Asked Questions

How can I volunteer with Muzita Legacy?

Volunteering is simple! Contact us through our volunteer email or use the contact form. We'll schedule an orientation to match your skills and interests with our current needs. Whether you can help weekly or occasionally, your contribution makes a difference.

How can I donate to support your work?

You can donate through our website, bank transfer, or contact us directly. Your donations support food aid, health monitoring, caregiver training, and community outreach programs. Every contribution helps elderly community members live with dignity.

Who can access your services?

Our services are available to elderly individuals in Mashonaland East and surrounding areas who need support with health monitoring, companionship, food security, or caregiver assistance. We prioritize vulnerable seniors and those with limited family support.

How do you partner with other organizations?

We welcome partnerships with healthcare providers, community organizations, businesses, and government agencies. Partners can provide services, resources, funding, or volunteer support. Contact us to discuss how we can work together to expand elderly care.
