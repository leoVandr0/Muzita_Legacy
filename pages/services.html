Our Activities

Comprehensive support services designed to meet the unique needs of elderly community members. We combine community engagement with compassionate support to ensure dignity and quality of life.

Establishing Support Groups

We create and facilitate support groups for elderly individuals and their caregivers to share experiences, receive emotional support, and build community connections.

  • Peer support networks
  • Caregiver sharing circles
  • Monthly group meetings
  • Community building activities

Providing Home-Based Care Services

Our dedicated team provides regular home visits to elderly community members who need assistance with daily living, health monitoring, and companionship.

  • Regular home visits
  • Health monitoring
  • Personal care assistance
  • Companionship and emotional support

Capacity Building for Caregivers

We provide comprehensive training programs for family caregivers and community leaders to improve their skills in elderly care and support.

  • Caregiver training workshops
  • Skills development programs
  • Best practices education
  • Ongoing support and mentorship

Community Outreach and Engagements

We actively engage communities to raise awareness about elderly care issues and mobilize resources to support our senior population.

  • Community awareness campaigns
  • Elderly rights advocacy
  • Resource mobilization
  • Partnership development

Health Support Services

We coordinate healthcare support for elderly community members, including medication reminders, health checks, and connection to medical services.

  • Health monitoring
  • Medication reminders
  • Medical appointment coordination
  • Nutrition and wellness support

Social Connection Programs

We create opportunities for elderly individuals to maintain social connections and participate in meaningful community activities.

  • Social activities coordination
  • Community event participation
  • Inter-generational programs
  • Recreational opportunities

Additional Services

Comprehensive support for every aspect of senior care

Nutritional Care

Customized meal plans designed by our nutritionist to meet dietary needs, manage chronic conditions, and provide enjoyable dining experiences.

Family Support

Counseling, education, and regular communication to help families navigate the challenges of having a loved one in care.

24/7 Nursing

Round-the-clock nursing care ensures immediate response to medical needs and continuous monitoring of resident health.

Social Activities

Engaging recreational and social programs designed to stimulate cognitive function, encourage socialization, and bring joy to daily life.
